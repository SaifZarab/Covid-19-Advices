Tips to minimize the chances of getting infected by Covid-19

Wash hands with soap and water after touching animal and animal products.

When coughing and sneezing cover mouth with flexed elbow or tissue.

Avoid travelling if you have a fever or cough.

If you have a fever,cough and difficulty breathing seek medical care early.

If you are coughing or sneezing,wear a mask and must know how to use it.Dispose of it properly.

Eat only well cooked homemade food.

Keep physical distance of at least 2 metres from the others even if they don't appear to be sick.
